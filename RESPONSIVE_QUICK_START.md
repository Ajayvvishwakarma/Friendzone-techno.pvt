## ? FAQs

### Q: Will this work on older phones?
A: Yes! It supports devices from 320px (very old phones) up to 1920px+

### Q: Do I need to change my HTML?
A: No! Only CSS was modified. HTML structure stays the same.

### Q: Will dark mode still work?
A: Yes! Dark mode works perfectly on all screen sizes.

### Q: Do forms work on mobile?
A: Yes! Forms are optimized for mobile with touch-friendly inputs.

### Q: Can I customize the breakpoints?
A: Yes! All breakpoints are in the `@media` queries in `style.css`

### Q: Does this affect performance?
A: No! All changes are CSS-only. Zero performance impact.

### Q: Is it accessible?
A: Yes! Proper touch targets (44px+) and good contrast throughout.
