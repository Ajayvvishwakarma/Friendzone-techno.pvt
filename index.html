❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information? ▼
Go to Dashboard → Profile Information card → Click "Edit Profile" button. Fill in your details and click "Save Changes".
How do I reset my password? ▼
On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your email. We'll send you a reset link to your email address.
How do I upload a profile photo? ▼
Go to Dashboard → Profile Information card → Click "Upload Photo". You can drag and drop or browse your computer for an image.
How do I change my email address? ▼
Go to Settings → Account Settings → Click "Change" next to Email Address. Verify your new email and confirm the change.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols. Your data is protected with SSL/TLS encryption.

ℹ️ About Frontend Data Storage

What is localStorage? Browser's local storage that persists even after closing the browser
Data Stored: User registrations, profile data, settings, and preferences
Access: Open browser DevTools (F12) → Application → localStorage
Persistence: Data stays until manually cleared or browser data is deleted
